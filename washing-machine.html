What We Do

Comprehensive Laundry Solutions

We service all Semi-Automatic, Top-Load, and Front-Load washing machines from brands like LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, IFB, and Bosch.

Drum & Motor Repair

Fixing issues where the drum is not spinning, making loud vibrating noises, or completely stuck due to bearing or motor failure.

Drainage & Leakage Fix

Clearing clogged drain pumps, replacing damaged drain pipes, and fixing water leaking from the bottom of the machine.

PCB & Error Codes

Expert diagnostics for dead machines, blinking display lights, or specific digital error codes requiring PCB board repair or replacement.

Door Lock & Gasket

Replacing faulty door interlocking switches, fixing jammed front-load doors, and replacing moldy or torn rubber gaskets.
