What We Do

Comprehensive Chimney Services

We service, repair, and install auto-clean and manual chimneys from top brands like Elica, Faber, Hindware, Glen, and Sunflame.

Deep Chemical Cleaning

Complete dismantling and chemical washing of the motor, blower, and baffle filters to remove thick, stubborn oil and grease buildup.

Low Suction Repair

Diagnosing and fixing weak smoke extraction by repairing the exhaust motor, clearing duct blockages, or replacing damaged blower fans.

PCB & Touch Panel

Expert repair for unresponsive touch controls, broken push buttons, or malfunctioning auto-clean heating elements requiring motherboard fixes.

Noise & Vibration Fix

Resolving annoying rattling sounds or heavy vibrations by realigning the motor, tightening mounting brackets, and balancing the blower.

Oil Dripping Issues

Fixing issues where dirty oil leaks back onto your stovetop by cleaning clogged internal oil trays and repairing the auto-clean mechanism.

Installation & Ducting

Professional wall-mount and island chimney installation, including precise core cutting and setup of flexible aluminium exhaust ducting pipes.

Preventive Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Avoid sudden breakdowns. We offer affordable annual maintenance contracts for all your heavy appliances to ensure they run efficiently year-round.
