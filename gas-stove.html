What We Do

Comprehensive Stove & Hob Services

We service all configurations from 2-burner to 5-burner hobs and cooktops from top brands like Glen, Elica, Prestige, Faber, and Pigeon.

Low Flame Issue

Cleaning clogged burner ports, adjusting gas regulators, and clearing venturi tubes to restore the powerful blue flame needed for efficient cooking.

Yellow or Orange Flame

Addressing dangerous, incomplete combustion that causes soot buildup. We adjust the air-to-gas ratio to return your burners to a safe blue burn.

Persistent Clicking

Fixing issues where the igniter keeps clicking even after the burner is lit, or when the automatic ignition refuses to spark at all.

Gas Leakage Detection

Using professional electronic leak detectors to identify smell origins. We safely replace faulty gas valves, hoses, regulators, and connectors.

Burner & Valve Replacement

Replacing warped burner caps, faulty brass jets, control knobs, and sticky or leaking gas valves with brand-original spare parts.

Deep Cleaning & Setup

Thorough cleaning to remove stubborn grease inside the hob unit, ensuring smooth knob operation and optimal safety performance.

Preventive Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Avoid sudden breakdowns. We offer affordable annual maintenance contracts for all your heavy appliances to ensure they run efficiently year-round.
