What We Do

Comprehensive AC Services

We service all types of Window and Split Air Conditioners from top brands like LG, Daikin, Voltas, Samsung, and more.

Deep Jet Cleaning

High-pressure water jet cleaning of the indoor cooling coil, outdoor condenser, and filters to restore maximum cooling efficiency and air quality.

Gas Leak Fix & Refill

Complete leak identification, nitrogen testing, vacuuming, and fresh refrigerant (R32, R410A, R22) refilling by weight for perfect cooling.

PCB & Electrical Repair

Expert diagnostics for non-starting ACs, display errors, capacitor replacements, and advanced indoor/outdoor PCB board level repairs.

Installation & Setup

Professional split/window AC installation with proper insulation, core-cutting, and copper piping setup to prevent future gas leaks.

Water Leakage Fix

Clearing blocked drainage pipes, adjusting indoor unit tilt, and fixing overflowing drain trays to stop indoor water dripping.

Motor & Fan Repair

Resolving loud operational noises by repairing or replacing faulty indoor blower motors and outdoor condenser fan blades.
