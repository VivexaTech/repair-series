What We Do

Comprehensive Hob Services

We service all 3, 4, and 5-burner built-in glass and stainless steel hobs from top brands like Faber, Glen, Elica, Bosch, and Hindware.

Auto-Ignition Repair

Fixing issues with continuous clicking, dead spark plugs, battery replacements, and damaged ignition wiring systems specific to built-in hobs.

Uneven & Low Flame

Cleaning clogged multi-ring brass burners and fine-tuning gas jets to ensure a powerful, balanced blue flame across all cooking zones.

Stuck Knobs & Valves

Repairing or replacing jammed control knobs and faulty European valves (like SABAF) that make it difficult to adjust or turn off the flame.

Concealed Gas Leaks

Expert detection and sealing of gas leaks in the internal piping, under-counter connections, and faulty regulators to ensure absolute kitchen safety.

Glass Top & Seal Service

Inspecting the alignment of your glass hob, fixing loose burner rings, and replacing worn-out rubber seals that prevent water from seeping inside.

Hob Deep Cleaning

Safe lifting of the hob to remove years of accumulated grease, spilled milk, and debris from the internal chassis, extending the appliance's lifespan.

Preventive Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Avoid sudden breakdowns. We offer affordable annual maintenance contracts for all your heavy appliances to ensure they run efficiently year-round.
