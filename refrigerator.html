What We Do

Comprehensive Fridge Services

We service all major brands including LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, Haier, Godrej, and Bosch, using only certified OEM spare parts.

Cooling Issues & Gas Refill

Complete leak identification, nitrogen testing, vacuuming, and exact-weight refilling of R134a or R600a refrigerant to restore optimal cooling.

Compressor Replacement

Diagnostics for dead or frequently tripping compressors. We offer professional replacement with brand-original parts and dedicated warranty.

PCB & Inverter Repair

Advanced component-level repair for completely dead refrigerators, display malfunctions, or blinking error lights on modern inverter models.

Thermostat & Sensors

Resolving extreme overcooling (heavy ice buildup) or undercooling by replacing faulty bi-metal thermostats, timers, and defrost sensors.

Water Leakage & Blockage

Clearing blocked defrost drains and fixing water leaking from the bottom, inside the fresh food compartment, or overflowing drain trays.

Door Gasket Replacement

Replacing torn, loose, or hardened magnetic door rubber seals to prevent cooling loss, stop condensation, and reduce electricity bills.
